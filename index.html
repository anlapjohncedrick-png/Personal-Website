LIFE



My name is John Cedrick Anlap, born on December 12, 2005, in Bacoor, Cavite. Growing up, I was surrounded by the sounds of daily life - my father, Jonathan, driving his tricycle, and my mother, Gina, working hard as a housewife.



I'm the second of four siblings, each with their own unique personality. My older sister, Stacy, is a trailblazer - adventurous, hardworking, and independent. My younger siblings, John Nathaniel and John Daniel, are a dynamic duo - Nathaniel, the gamer, and Daniel, the creative woodworker.



Then my mom decided to look for work to provide for us. She raised us all four with the help of my mother's family. Then a few years after, our life become a little better. My sister already have a job to support her study and become independent. I, on the other hand, are already in high school and doing good in my study. Then, pandemic came and everyone are forced to stay inside the house. Due to this, I was forced to take modular class but sadly become depressed and forced to stop my study so that I can heal through medication.



I got back at school a year later and continue to excel in school after being at the lowest point in my life.



Now, after all that I have been through, I still do my best to do well in my study so that I can get a job and help my family in the future. So always think positive, pray and be strong no matter what happens. Because tomorrow remains uncertain, full of possibilities waiting to uncover.
